RECIPE


    • 1 pound (450g) of ground pork (you can also use a mixture of pork and veal)
    • 1/4 cup (60ml) of cold water
    • 2 teaspoons of salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon of white pepper
    • 1/4 teaspoon of ground mace
    • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
    • Natural hog casings (for stuffing the sausage)


PROCEDURE


    Prepare the Casings:
    • Soak the natural hog casings in warm water for about 30 minutes to soften them. Rinse them thoroughly inside and out to remove any salt.

    Mix the Ingredients:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, cold water, salt, white pepper, mace, and nutmeg. Mix thoroughly until the ingredients are well combined. You can also use a stand mixer with a paddle attachment for this step.

    Prepare the Sausage Stuffer:
    • If you have a sausage stuffer, attach the appropriate-sized stuffing tube for your casings. If not, you can use a pastry bag with a large round tip.

    Stuff the Casings:
    • Carefully slide the soaked hog casings onto the stuffing tube, leaving about 6 inches of casing hanging off the end. Tie a knot at the other end of the casing.
    • Begin feeding the sausage mixture into the casing, being careful not to overfill. Leave some space between the sausages and twist them into links (typically 4-6 inches long). Pinch and twist in one direction to seal each link.

    Let the Sausages Rest:
    • Place the freshly made Bratwurst in the refrigerator for a few hours or overnight. This allows the flavors to meld and the casings to dry slightly.

    Cook the Bratwurst:
    • You can grill, pan-fry, or bake the Bratwurst. If grilling, cook them over medium heat until they're browned and cooked through (about 15-20 minutes). If pan-frying, use a bit of oil and cook over medium heat until browned on all sides and cooked through. Baking is an option too; preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake for about 20-25 minutes.

    Serve:
    • Serve the Bratwurst with your favorite condiments like mustard, sauerkraut, and buns or alongside traditional sides like potato salad.
